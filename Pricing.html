How these prices were set

Independent patient advocates commonly charge ~$100–$500/hr, with many real-world services listing rates around $150–$200/hr. Salary benchmarks for oncology/patient navigators often translate to ~$29–$49/hr (employed roles), before overhead and business costs in private practice. Given oncology specialization, PA-C credentials, and 10 years’ experience, $180/hr is a fair-market midpoint; bundles reflect typical time blocks for new diagnoses and active treatment.
