About Me

I’m Grace Lyles, PA-C. I guide patients and families through the most confusing parts of cancer care—between doctor visits.

I am Grace Lyles PA-C. I have worked in internal medicine and oncology for the past 10 years. I have worked inside research and non-research settings. Last year my husband was diagnosed with thyroid cancer and I realized just how daunting this would have been without the knowledge and resources we both have within the medical system. I am here to help you answer your questions between your doctor visits and to help you find the care that you need. Cancer care has come a long way even in just the past 10 years. But that often makes decisions of treatment more complicated. I can help you understand your diagnosis, breakdown the different types of genetic testing available, and help you think through all those questions you want to ask.

“Having cancer is hard, getting what you need out of the medical system doesn’t have to be.”

My role complements—never replaces—your oncology team. I do not diagnose, treat, or prescribe; I help you prepare, understand, and decide with confidence.

  • Focus: medical literacy, genetic & biomarker testing, treatment decision support
  • Approach: calm, practical, evidence-aware, and family-centered
  • Availability: secure video, phone, and email between visits
